If you encounter a situation where your dog is injured
or in distress, follow these basic first aid steps: Stay Calm: Your dog can sense your emotions.
Stay calm to keep your dog as calm as possible.
Assess the Situation: Determine the nature and severity of the injury or distress.
Control Bleeding: Apply gentle pressure to stop any bleeding.
Use a clean cloth or bandage.
Clear Airways: If your dog is choking, perform the Heimlich maneuver or
try to clear the obstruction from their mouth.
Perform CPR: If your dog is not breathing
perform cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR).
Immobilize Injuries: If there are broken bones or
suspected fractures, try to immobilize the area with splints or bandages.
Seek Veterinary Help: Even if the injury seems minor,
it's best to consult a vet to ensure proper care.
